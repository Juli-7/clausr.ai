# UN R83 — Exhaust Emissions (Light Vehicles)

## §5.2 CO2 Emission Limits

Type-approval CO2 emission limits for light-duty vehicles based on reference mass:
- Category M1 (passenger vehicles): 130 g/km (NEDC), 95 g/km (WLTP)
- Vehicles with reference mass > 1,305 kg receive mass-based derogation: CO2 limit = 130 + 0.0457 × (mass − 1,305)

## §5.4 NOx Emission Limits

NOx emission limits per Euro stage:
- Euro 6: 60 mg/km (diesel), 60 mg/km (petrol)
- Euro 6d: 60 mg/km (diesel), 60 mg/km (petrol) with RDE conformity factor ≤ 1.43 (Euro 6d-temp) or ≤ 1.0 (Euro 6d)

## §5.5 Particulate Limits

Particulate number (PN) limits:
- Euro 6: 6.0 × 10^11/km (direct injection petrol), 6.0 × 10^11/km (diesel)
- Particulate mass (PM): 4.5 mg/km (all fuels)

## §6.1 OBD Requirements

On-Board Diagnostic (OBD) system requirements:
- OBD threshold limits for NOx: 0.12 g/km (positive ignition), 0.17 g/km (compression ignition)
- OBD threshold for PM: 12 mg/km (all)
- OBD must monitor catalyst efficiency, oxygen sensors, EGR system, misfire detection, and evaporative system purge

## §8.1 Durability Requirements

Emission durability periods (minimum):
- Category M1: 160,000 km or 5 years
- Deterioration factors apply at 80,000 km and 160,000 km intervals
