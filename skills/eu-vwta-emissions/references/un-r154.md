# UN R154 — Worldwide Harmonised Light Vehicle Emissions (WLTP)

## §6.2 WLTP Cycle Requirements

The WLTP test cycle consists of four phases:
- Low (urban, ≤ 56.5 km/h average speed, 589 s duration)
- Medium (suburban, 76.6 km/h average, 433 s)
- High (extra-urban, 97.4 km/h average, 455 s)
- Extra-High (motorway, 131.3 km/h average, 323 s)

Total test duration: 1,800 s, total distance: 23.27 km.

## §6.3 CO2 Measurement

CO2 emissions are declared per WLTP using the cycle-weighted average:
- Combined CO2 = 0.25 × CO2(Low) + 0.28 × CO2(Medium) + 0.33 × CO2(High) + 0.14 × CO2(Extra-High)
- Mass correction factor applies when vehicle mass differs from standard test mass

## §6.4 Test Mass

Test mass = kerb mass + 100 kg (driver + instrumentation) + 15% of payload for M1 vehicles.
All-wheel drive vehicles use the same chassis dynamometer settings as 2WD.
