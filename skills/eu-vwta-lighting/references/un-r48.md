# UN R48 — Installation of Lighting and Light-Signalling Devices

## §5.11 Automatic Levelling

If the vehicle is equipped with LED light sources, an automatic levelling device is mandatory. The system must compensate for static pitch variations within 30 seconds of vehicle loading changes. Static bending lamps are excluded from this requirement.

## §6.1 Number of Headlamps

Each vehicle shall be equipped with at least two headlamps emitting a main-beam and a passing-beam. Headlamps shall be symmetrically disposed about the longitudinal median plane.

## §6.2 Mounting Height

The height of headlamps shall be between 500 mm and 1,200 mm from the ground, measured to the centre of the illuminating surface of the headlamp.
