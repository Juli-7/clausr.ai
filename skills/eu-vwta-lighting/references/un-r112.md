# UN R112 — Headlamp Performance Requirements

## §5.3 Beam Cutoff

The vertical gradient of the cut-off line shall not exceed 0.57° (10% at 25m distance). Asymmetrical passing beam must produce a horizontal cut-off on the left and a 15° upward inclination on the right.

## §5.5 Colour Temperature

The correlated colour temperature of LED headlamp sources shall not exceed 6,000 K. Sources with CCT below 3,000 K are permitted only for fog lamps.
