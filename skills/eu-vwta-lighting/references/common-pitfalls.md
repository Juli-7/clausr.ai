# Common Pitfalls — EU VWTA Lighting

## Auto-Leveling Confusion

The most common mistake is forgetting that R48 §5.11 applies to ALL LED sources, not just main-beam LEDs. If ANY light source on the vehicle is LED, auto-leveling is mandatory.

## Mounting Height Measurement

Mounting height is measured from ground to the centre of the illuminating surface — NOT the bottom or top of the headlamp assembly. The centre is defined as the midpoint between the lowest and highest illuminated points.

## Beam Cutoff Measurement

Beam cutoff is measured at 25m distance on a vertical screen. The 0.57° gradient corresponds to approximately 250mm vertical change over 25m horizontal distance.

## Retrofit Considerations

Some manufacturers claim auto-leveling can be retrofitted after type approval. This is generally NOT permitted — the levelling device must be fitted at the time of type approval.
