# UN R13 — Braking Systems (Passenger & Light Commercial)

## §5.2 Service Brake Performance

Service brake performance requirements (Type-0 test, engine disconnected):
- Category M1: stopping distance ≤ 0.1 × v + v²/150 (where v is test speed in km/h)
- Equivalent to: MFDD ≥ 5.8 m/s²
- Test speed: 100 km/h (M1 vehicles)
- Pedal force: 65–500 N (M1)

## §5.3 Secondary (Emergency) Brake Performance

Secondary braking system must achieve:
- Stopping distance ≤ 0.1 × v + v²/65 (M1)
- Equivalent to: MFDD ≥ 2.9 m/s²
- Test speed: 80 km/h (half of max speed, capped at 80 km/h)
- Must be usable from the normal driving position without releasing the steering wheel

## §5.4 Parking Brake Performance

Parking brake requirements:
- Must hold vehicle stationary on 20% gradient (both uphill and downhill)
- For M1: maximum hand force 400 N, maximum foot force 500 N
- For vehicles with automatic transmission: parking brake must hold with gear in Park (P)

## §5.5 Brake Fade (Type-I Test)

Heated brake performance (Type-I test):
- 20 consecutive brake applications from 80% of max speed to 40% of max speed
- Interval between applications: 60 seconds
- After test: hot performance ≥ 60% of required cold performance (and ≥ 80% of Type-0 measured value)

## §5.6 ABS Requirements

Vehicles with anti-lock braking systems must:
- Achieve utilisation of adhesion ≥ 75% on high-friction and low-friction surfaces
- ABS must be operational on all wheels
- Control cycles must not lock any wheel during full ABS intervention
- For electric vehicles: ABS must function during regenerative braking transitions
