01 Risk Classification

The system is classified as high-risk under Article 6(2) of the EU AI Act, given its deployment in employment context Reg 6(2). The provider has submitted a S1.chunk-03 conformity assessment under Annex III. The technical documentation includes system architecture, training methodology, and performance benchmarks.

We reviewed the risk management process described in §9 of the technical report. The process identifies foreseeable risks associated with each operational phase and describes mitigation measures. However, the S1.chunk-07 documentation lacks explicit handling for edge cases involving demographic subgroups.

02 Data Governance

Training data provenance is documented for 94% of the dataset Art 10. The remaining 6% consists of publicly scraped data with incomplete attribution records. The provider has implemented data minimisation and purpose limitation controls in accordance with Art 10(3).

Bias detection analysis was performed on the validation set. Results indicate a deviation of ≤2.3% across demographic groups, which falls within the acceptable threshold defined in S2.chunk-11. Three data quality metrics were below the recommended minimum: completeness (92% vs ≥95%), timeliness (88% vs ≥90%), and consistency (94% vs ≥95%).
